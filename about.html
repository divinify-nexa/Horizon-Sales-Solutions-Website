Our Story

With People

We didn't set out to build another sales company. We set out to build a place where people could discover what they're truly capable of.

Who We Are

Born From a Belief in Potential

Horizon Sales & Solutions was founded with a simple but radical idea: that every person who walks through our doors has the potential to become a leader. Not someday. Starting now.

Based in Atlanta, GA, we partner with some of the nation's leading brands to deliver face-to-face customer acquisition campaigns. But what sets us apart isn't just our results — it's how we achieve them. Through mentorship, hands-on training, and a culture that genuinely celebrates growth.

Our team operates with the energy of a championship locker room and the precision of a boardroom. We show up every day to push boundaries — for our clients, for our communities, and for each other.

Our Foundation

The Values That Define Us

01

Unity

We rise together. At Horizon, there are no solo acts. Every win is shared, every challenge is faced as a team. We believe the strength of our collective is greater than any individual — and we show up for each other, every single day.

02

Student Mentality

Always learning, always growing. The moment you think you've figured it all out is the moment you stop getting better. We approach every day with curiosity, humility, and a hunger to improve — from the newest rep to the CEO.

03

1% Better Every Day

Small gains compound into wins. We don't chase overnight success — we chase daily progress. That extra rep, that harder conversation, that moment of discipline when no one's watching. It all adds up.

04

Gratitude

We celebrate how far we've come. It's easy to focus on what's next. But at Horizon, we make space to appreciate the journey — the lessons, the milestones, and the people who helped us get here.
